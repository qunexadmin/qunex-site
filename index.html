Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Qunex+ platform

Do you offer a free trial?

Yes— we offer a 14-day free trial with full platform access and no credit card required. You can explore all features and connect your marketing channels to see the power of unified analytics.

Which integrations are supported?

Qunex+ integrates with 15+ platforms including Meta (Facebook & Instagram), Google Ads, TikTok, Shopify, HubSpot, LinkedIn, YouTube, WordPress, and more. We're constantly adding new integrations based on customer needs.

How does Qunex+ ensure data security?

We use SOC 2 Type II aligned controls, end-to-end encryption for data in transit and at rest, and are GDPR compliant. Your marketing data is protected with enterprise-grade security measures and regular security audits.

Can I export my data from Qunex+?

Yes, you own your data and can export it anytime via CSV, Excel, or our comprehensive REST API. There's no vendor lock-in— your data remains yours and is always accessible.
